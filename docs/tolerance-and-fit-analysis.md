# Tolerance And Fit Analysis

Status: open-question analysis only. All clearances, fits, and tolerances are
`pending_measurement`; no numeric fit is fabrication authority.

## Critical Fits As Open Questions

| interface | why it matters | open fit/clearance question | evidence needed |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Key pin to key mortise | Controls wobble, friction, and repeatable touch. | How much side play permits free motion without visible racking? | Measured key sample or keyboard mockup. |
| Key tail to jack bottom | Converts key motion into jack lift. | What contact shape avoids binding while preserving repeatable lift? | Action-mule contact test. |
| Jack to guide slots | Controls vertical travel and return. | What clearance prevents binding across humidity changes while keeping plectrum alignment? | Guide material test and seasonal movement review. |
| Tongue pivot to jack body | Controls plectrum reset and pass-under. | What pivot fit resets reliably without chatter or delayed return? | Tongue-reset bench log. |
| Plectrum to string | Controls pluck strength, release, and wear. | What insertion depth and voicing shape are repeatable without over-driving the string? | Voicing trial after stringing assumptions are reviewed. |
| Damper to string | Controls release silence. | What damper contact stops the string without pulling it sharp or muting adjacent notes? | Damper mockup and listening/measurement notes. |
| Register slide to jack row | Selects or withdraws choirs. | How much slide travel and detent stability are needed for reliable on/off alignment? | Register-slide mockup. |
| Buff stop to string choir | Adds damping color. | What contact path is reversible and non-damaging? | Buff-stop material and engagement test. |
| Tuning pin to wrestplank | Holds string tension. | What material stack and drilling process hold tuning without splitting or creeping? | Specialist review and sample tests. |
| Bridge to soundboard | Transfers string energy. | What bridge/soundboard interface is structurally safe and acoustically useful? | Reviewed soundboard plan before layout. |
| Case joints to string load path | Keeps geometry stable. | Which joints resist string load and humidity movement without undocumented assumptions? | Structural review and measured drawings. |
| Service access to action | Enables regulation. | Can each jack, plectrum, damper, and register be reached without disassembly beyond planned maintenance? | Maintenance walkthrough on a mockup or CAD review. |

## Fit Review Rules

- Do not convert an open question into a clearance value without evidence.
- Treat historical examples, photos, and diagrams as reference-only until
  measured or reviewed.
- Record humidity, material direction, wear, and service access as fit risks,
  not polish items.
- Any future CAD or drawing that shows a fit must cite the measured or reviewed
  source row that controls it.
